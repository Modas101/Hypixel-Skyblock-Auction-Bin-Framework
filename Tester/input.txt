ionic bond|a strong attraction that forms betweeen oppositely charged ions
covalent compound|a compound that results when atoms of two or more elements bond covalently
covalent bond|a strong attraction between atoms that forms when atoms share valence electrons
molecule|a particle made up of two or more atoms bonded by covalent bonds
law of conservation of mass|in a chemical reaction the total mass of the substances used is equal to the total mass of the substances produced
chemical equation|a representation of a chemical reaction using words or chemical formulas
reactant|a substance that undergoes a chemical change
product|a new substance formed from a chemical reaction
coefficient|a number placed in front of a chemical formula in a balanced chemical equation to show the ratios of substances in reaction
